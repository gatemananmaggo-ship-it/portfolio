Smart Waste Management System Dashboard

The invention Smart Waste Management System is an IoT-based module which utilizes HC-SR04 ultrasonic sensor to monitor the exact fill level of garbage bins in real-time. With the help of Transceiver LoRa WAN (Transmits and Receive the data over frequencies like 433, 865 & 867 MHz in India), it transmits the most precise fill level of bin data to a centralized hub (admin dashboard) without even costing any transmission charges. This system maps the most optimized garbage collection routes by using OSRM (Open Source Routing Machine), which ensures preventing overflowing of garbage bins, ensures the public hygiene and resource management. The unique combination of low-power and secure transmission of data and effective routing exponentially reduces the operational cost and carbon emission.
